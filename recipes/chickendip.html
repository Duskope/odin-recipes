Buffalo Chicken Dip

A recipe for buffalo chicken dip, from Cafe Delites.

  • 1 tablespoon unsalted butter
  • 2 teaspoons unsalted garlic
  • 2 cups cooked chicken shredded
  • 1/2 cup Frank's Original Red Hot Sauce
  • 8 oz block cream cheese softened
  • 1/2 cup sour cream
  • 1/2 cup shredded white cheddar cheese
  • 1/4 cup shredded american cheddar cheese
  • 1/4 cup crumbled blue cheese
  • 2 teaspoons green onions sliced

  1. Preheat oven to 375F
  2. In a cast iron skillet, melt butter over medium heat. Saute garlic for 30 seconds. Add chicken and hot sauce and simmer until sauce has thickened
  3. Reduce heat to low and stir cream cheese. Remove from heat, and stir in sour cream and top with both cheddar cheeses
  4. Bake until bubbling around edges and cheese is melted (Aprox. 10 minutes).
  5. Garnish with blue cheese and green onions. Serve and enjoy.
