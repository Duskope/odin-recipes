Dale's Marinated Grilled Steak

This is a very simple recipe, from myself, to cook a good steak on a charcoal grill with Dale's seasoning.

Ingredients:

  • Steak of choice, NY strip reccomended
  • Bottle of Dale's seasoning

Instructions:

  1. Lay steaks beside eachother in container or dish of choice. Fill container with Dale's until steaks are covered. Cover container. Put in refridgerator and let sit for at least 1 hour.
  2. Light coals in grill. Wait until coals are greyed over and burning hot, then spread coals. Heat grill to 350F.
  3. Put steaks on grill and cook for 8 minutes on each side. Keep grill at 350F.
  4. Serve and enjoy.
